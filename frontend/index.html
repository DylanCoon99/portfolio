About Me

Hi! My name is Dylan Coon and I am from Erie, Pennsylvania. I’m a software engineer with a strong foundation in computer science and a passion for solving complex problems. I graduated from Penn State University with a degree in Computer Science and a minor in Mathematics, where I built a deep understanding of algorithms, systems, and logical thinking — skills I apply daily in my work and personal projects.

Currently, I’m a software engineer at Iron Mountain, where I work on an application support team. I spend most of my time writing Python scripts to automate workflows. I enjoy working with data, writing clean APIs, and diving into technical challenges. My main interests lie in backend development, distributed systems, and machine learning.

Outside of work, I like to be outside and spend time with my dog Yankee. I am also an avid rock climber — I love the mental and physical puzzle that each climb presents. It’s a great complement to my love of problem-solving in software.
